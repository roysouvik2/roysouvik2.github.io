This page lists the various software packages I have created with my co-developers


OFDS

Optical flow DEAL.II solver

A 2D solver for computing optical fluid flow velocity of a given image sequence (with Praveen C. and A. S. Vasudeva Murthy)


CERTS

Circular-Elliptic Radon Transform Solver

A 2D solver for computing reconstructing an image from its circular and elliptic Radon transforms (with V. P. Krishnan, Praveen C. and A. S. Vasudeva Murthy)


BRT_SOLVER

Broken Ray Radon Transform Solver

A 2D solver for computing reconstructing an image from its broken ray Radon transforms (with Gaik Ambartsoumian)


ADICC

Alternate Direction Implicit Chang Cooper solver

A 2D solver for implementing the coupled alternate direction implicit Chang Cooper scheme for stochastic ITO processes in Fokker-Planck framework (with Alfio Borzi and Mario Annunziato)


DGVORVEL

DG solver for vorticity-velocity formulation of incompressible flows.

A 2D/3D DG solver for vorticity-velocity formulation of incompressible Euler and Navier Stokes equations (with Praveen C.)


FENICS

List of FENICS codes for various applications.



COLON CANCER (NIH-NCI PROJECT)

Parameter estimation and sensitivity analysis codes for a model of colon cancer

Parameter estimation based on optimization method and sensitivity analysis using novel Latin hypercube sampling and Partial Rank Correlation Coefficient Method for a QSP model of colon cancer (with Suvra Pal, Achyuth Manoj, Susanth Kakarla, Juan Villegas and Mesfer Alajmi)
