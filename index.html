Souvik Roy

Assistant Professor
University of Texas at Arlington, USA

Hi! I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the Department of Mathematics, University of Texas at Arlington . My primary research area is in the field of inverse problems in medical imaging, stochastic models for cancer and disease dynamics, and optimal treatment assessments. I also work in other interdisciplinary areas like developing optimization algorithms for cure rate models and mathematical methods for signal processing in chromatography.

Funding from the NSF awards DMS-2212938, DMS-2309491, DMS-2230790; NIH awards R21CA242933, R15GM150091; and UTA IRP and REP grants are gratefully acknowledged.
