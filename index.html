Assistant Professor
University of Texas, Arlington, USA
Hi! I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the Department of Mathematics, University of Texas, Arlington. My primary research area is in the field of inverse problems in medical imaging, stochastic models for cancer and disease dynamics, and optimal treatment assessments. I also work in other interdisciplinary areas like developing optimization algorithms for cire rate models.

Funding from the NSF grant DMS-2212938, NCI-NIH grant R21CA242933, and UTA IRP and REP grants are gratefully acknowledged.
