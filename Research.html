Research interests


  • Inverse problems

    My primary area of interest is in the field of inverse problems in fluid flows and medical imaging. For inverse problems in fluid flows, I am interested in determining the flow properties like velocity, vorticity, density, given image data which is advected by the fluid flow field. I study both the theoretical and computational aspects of such models. For inverse problems in medical imaging, I develop numerical algorithms for reconstructing an image in 2D/3D from its Radon tansform (Circular, Elliptical, Broken ray, Spherical). I focuss mainly on the robustness, accuracy and complexity of such algorithms.

  • Observers for compressible flows

    I am working on developing observers for tracking image data advected by compressible flows. Given observations in full/partial spatial doamin, I am studying the behaviour of the observer system and look at determining decay rates for the convergence of solutions of the observer system to the actual solution.

  • Discontinuous Galerkin (DG) schemes

    I am interested in developing DG schemes for 2D/3D incompressible flows. I study theoretical properties like stability, error estimates of the DG scheme and validate it numerically using FEM packages like FENICS, DEAL.II, COMSOL with the aid of visual plotting softwares like PARAVIEW, GMSH, VISIT

  • PDE optimal control frameworks for stochastic processes

    I am also working on optimal control of motion governed by stochastic processes in the framework of PDE's. My goal is to develop suitable optimal control framework, look at theoretical existence and uniqueness of such optimal controls, develop higher order positive schemes for the forward model and validate the framework using suitable numerical experiments.

  • Shape optimization

    I am also involved in determining the best shape among a class of punctured non-concentric domains so as to minimize the first Dirichlet eigenvalue. Such problems are of great interest in determining the shape of musical instruments or even in placement of obstacles. I use both theoretical as well as numerical methods for solving such problems.
